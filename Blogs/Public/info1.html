Importance Of Science

Hello Friends! Did you know that the human body glows and shines just like a diamond! We do not notice it because it is not visible to our naked eyes. Today I am here to speak on the topic - Importance of Science. But firstly, what is science?

Science is the study of laws of nature, animals, human beings, the environment, plants, space, and many more for their characteristics. It is also the study of natural and artificial life found on the earth. Science has done wonders for all of us and our lives. Chemistry, Biology Technology, Physics, Astrology and Astronomy are some of the parts of Science.

It has discovered laws and has invented many new products. Engineers, doctors, and scientists have made many gadgets for use at homes, in offices, or factories. Without science, it is difficult to imagine what our life would be like.

Science continues to make our life easier, healthier, and safe. In the future, science will advance more in each area and will help to improve the standard of our life in each small part. This is why we need to understand the importance of science.

Thank You!
