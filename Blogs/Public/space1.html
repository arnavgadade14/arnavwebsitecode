Sun, The Heart Of Life

Do you know that the Sun is very important in our life? Sun is important in our life because it produces light, heat, energy and everything. We also gain Vitamin D. We need light because if there is no light then we will not see anything at day. We need heat because if there is no heat then not only Earth, also other planets will be cold. We also need energy because if there is no energy then we will not be strong. And it is very risky for us.

The Sun is the star at the center of our Solar System. It is a perfect sphere hot plasma. It is gigantic. It is 1 100 000 km long. Sun is 100 times as wide as Earth, and 93 million miles far away Earth. Sun is also called Sol. The Sun has formed 4.5 billion years ago. And the Sun is 4.603 billion years old.
